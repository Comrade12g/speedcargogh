Air freight from China to Ghana

When sea freight is too slow, fly it. Daily consolidations from Guangzhou (CAN), Shenzhen (SZX), Shanghai (PVG) and Hong Kong (HKG) into Kotoka International Airport (ACC), cleared and delivered in 5–9 days door-to-door.

Get an air-freight quote View air rates

Express air — 5–7 days

Best for samples, urgent stock, replacement parts, electronics. Cargo flies on the next available scheduled freighter or belly-hold flight from China to Accra.

  • Daily cut-off in Guangzhou / Shenzhen
  • Door-to-door 5–7 working days
  • Per-kg pricing with chargeable weight
  • Best for shipments under 500 kg

Standard air — 7–9 days

Cost-optimised air freight using consolidated cargo flights via Addis Ababa, Istanbul or Dubai. Same care, lower rate, slightly longer transit.

  • Twice-weekly cut-off
  • Door-to-door 7–9 working days
  • Tiered rate (cheaper at 100kg+, 300kg+, 500kg+)
  • Best for 50 kg – 1500 kg

Chargeable weight

Airlines charge the higher of actual weight or volumetric weight. Volumetric = (L × W × H in cm) ÷ 6000. We confirm chargeable weight before booking so there are no surprises at delivery.

Restricted cargo

  • Loose lithium batteries — case by case
  • Liquids, aerosols, magnetic items — declared in advance
  • Branded goods — proof of authorisation required
  • Powders & cosmetics — MSDS document needed

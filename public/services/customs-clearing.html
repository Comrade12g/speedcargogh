Service · Customs clearing

Customs clearing in Ghana — Tema port & Kotoka airport

Our licensed customs house agents handle every step of the GRA Integrated Customs Management System (ICUMS): IDF, CTN, classification, duty calculation, payment, examination and final release.

What we handle

  • IDF (Import Declaration Form) preparation
  • CTN (Cargo Tracking Note) processing
  • HS code classification and duty estimation
  • ICUMS declaration and payment
  • Physical examination escort at Tema / KIA
  • Release order and gate-out

Documents we need from you

  • Commercial invoice
  • Packing list
  • Bill of Lading / Airway Bill
  • Importer TIN and Ghana Card
  • Permits where applicable (FDA, Standards, EPA)

Typical clearing time

From manifest registration to gate-out:

  • Air freight (Kotoka): 1–2 working days
  • LCL sea freight (Tema): 3–5 working days
  • FCL sea freight (Tema): 2–4 working days

Times depend on inspection regime and duty payment timing.

Duty estimator

Ghana applies import duty (0–20%), VAT (15%), NHIL (2.5%), GETFund (2.5%), ECOWAS levy (0.5%) and other small fees on the CIF value. Share your invoice and we'll calculate landed cost before you commit.

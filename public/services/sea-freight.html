Sea freight from China to Ghana

Weekly LCL groupage and full container (20ft / 40ft / 40HC) sailings from every major Chinese port to Tema. Door-to-door pricing, customs clearing and Accra-area delivery handled by one team.

Get a sea-freight quote View sailing schedule

LCL — Less than container load

Best for shipments 0.5 m³ to 12 m³. We consolidate your cargo with other Ghana-bound boxes in our Yiwu and Guangzhou warehouses and sail weekly to Tema.

  • Transit time: 30–38 days port-to-port
  • Free storage in China up to 14 days
  • Combined commercial invoice and packing list
  • Per-CBM pricing — see rate sheet

FCL — Full container load

20ft, 40ft and 40ft high-cube containers loaded at your supplier or at our warehouse. Best for furniture, machinery, building materials and large retail consignments.

  • Transit time: 28–34 days port-to-port
  • Shipper-owned or carrier-owned containers
  • Tema port clearing, demurrage management
  • Inland trucking anywhere in Ghana

What's included

  • Pickup from your Chinese supplier
  • Quality and quantity check at our warehouse
  • Export documentation and Bill of Lading
  • Ocean freight to Tema port
  • GRA customs clearing
  • Delivery to your Accra / Kumasi / Takoradi address

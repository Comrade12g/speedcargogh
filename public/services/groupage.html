Port-to-port LCL

If you have your own clearing agent in Ghana, we'll quote port-to-port only — China origin to Tema port arrival. You handle clearing and pickup, we handle the rest.

Double customs clearing

We handle both China export clearance and Ghana import clearance. The container is sealed at our China warehouse and only opened after Tema clearing — your cargo is never touched in transit.

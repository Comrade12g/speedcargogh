Warehousing & consolidation

Buy from many suppliers, ship as one shipment. Our warehouses in Yiwu, Guangzhou, Shenzhen and Tema consolidate, inspect, repack and dispatch on your schedule.

China warehouses

  • Yiwu — small commodities, fashion, accessories
  • Guangzhou — wholesale fabric, hair, cosmetics, bags
  • Shenzhen / Dongguan — electronics, gadgets, tools
  • Shanghai & Ningbo — machinery, industrial supplies

What we do on arrival

  • Receive cargo from your supplier
  • Photograph each parcel
  • Check quantity vs. packing list
  • Visible quality check (no inspection on sealed boxes)
  • Weigh and measure
  • Repack & consolidate to reduce CBM

Free storage window

14 days free storage in our China warehouses while you consolidate suppliers. After 14 days, ¥1.5 / CBM / day applies.

Tema warehouse

Bonded and unbonded storage at our Tema facility for distribution to retailers, kiosks, salons and traders across Ghana. Pick-and-pack and palletised dispatch available.

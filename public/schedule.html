Booking

Send packing list and supplier details before the cut-off. Speed Cargo will confirm the sailing and issue a booking number.

Documents

Commercial invoice, packing list, BL/AWB and CCVO are required for clearing at Tema. Speed Cargo handles customs.

Clearing & release

Typical Tema clearing is 5–10 working days after vessel arrival, depending on duty assessment and inspection.

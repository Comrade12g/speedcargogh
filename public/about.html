Company · 公司简介

Safe, professional, efficient cargo from China to Africa.

Speed Cargo is an international import & export agency moving commercial cargo, personal imports and full containers between China and Africa, with offices in Yiwu, Shenzhen, Ghana and Congo.

安全、专业、高效 — 货物跟踪 / 精细化服务 / 高品质交付。 在义乌、深圳、加纳和刚果设有分公司。

Why Speed Cargo · 关于我们

20+ years moving cargo to Africa and the Middle East.

Established in 2004 and approved by China's Ministry of Commerce, the company has built stable trade relationships with buyers in more than 100 countries and ships 800+ containers a year. With close supplier partnerships across China and four overseas branches, Speed Cargo is trusted by importers, traders and online sellers as a reliable end-to-end shipping partner.

公司成立于2004年，是经国家商务部批准的国际海运进出口代理企业， 已和全球100多个国家和地区的买家建立稳定贸易合作关系， 年出货量达800余集装箱。在义乌、深圳、加纳、刚果均设有分公司， 以诚信为本，在客户和供应商心中赢得了良好口碑。

Our strengths · 公司实力

Safe, stable and well-resourced.

Safety advantage 安全优势

Goods are handled by Speed Cargo staff from the China-side warehouse all the way to destination — cargo never changes hands with third parties.

Stability advantage 稳定优势

Strong booking volume secures stable space on every major lane. Air departs daily, sea containers are loaded every day.

Financial strength 资金优势

Well-capitalised operations and complete after-sales support — no surprises between booking, sailing and release.

Network advantage 综合优势

Four branches in China and Africa, dedicated stalls and warehouses in major wholesale markets — 20 years deep in Africa & Middle East trade.

Our branches · 海内外分公司

Four offices linking China to West & Central Africa.

China · 中国

Yiwu Branch 义乌分公司

Supplier pickup and consolidation at the Yiwu International Trade Market — sourcing, quality checks and warehouse-to-warehouse moves for shop owners and traders.

China · 中国

Shenzhen Branch 深圳分公司

Sea freight operations out of Yantian and Shekou — FCL container planning, LCL consolidation and electronics-grade air cargo from Shenzhen Bao'an.

Ghana · 加纳

Ghana Branch 加纳分公司

Accra-based clearing and warehouse release at Tema Port. Two warehouses in Laterbiokorshie and Spintex Manet for cargo pickup and final delivery.

Congo · 刚果

Congo Branch 刚果分公司

On-the-ground team in Pointe-Noire handling clearance and last-mile distribution across the Congo basin, with warehouse storage for collection or onward delivery.

Company · 公司简介

Safe, professional, efficient cargo from China to Africa.

Speed Cargo is an international import & export agency moving commercial cargo, personal imports and full containers from China to Ghana, with offices in Yiwu, Guangzhou and Ghana.

安全、专业、高效 — 货物跟踪 / 精细化服务 / 高品质交付。 在义乌、广州和加纳设有分公司。

Why Speed Cargo · 关于我们

20+ years moving cargo to Africa and the Middle East.

Established in 2004 and approved by China's Ministry of Commerce, the company has built stable trade relationships with buyers in more than 100 countries and ships 800+ containers a year. With close supplier partnerships across China and offices in Yiwu, Guangzhou and Ghana, Speed Cargo is trusted by importers, traders and online sellers as a reliable end-to-end shipping partner.

公司成立于2004年，是经国家商务部批准的国际海运进出口代理企业， 已和全球100多个国家和地区的买家建立稳定贸易合作关系， 年出货量达800余集装箱。在义乌、广州和加纳均设有分公司， 以诚信为本，在客户和供应商心中赢得了良好口碑。

Our strengths · 公司实力

Safe, stable and well-resourced.

Safety advantage 安全优势

Goods are handled by Speed Cargo staff from the China-side warehouse all the way to destination — cargo never changes hands with third parties.

Stability advantage 稳定优势

Strong booking volume secures stable space on every major lane. Sea containers are loaded every day.

Financial strength 资金优势

Well-capitalised operations and complete after-sales support — no surprises between booking, sailing and release.

Network advantage 综合优势

Three branches across China and Ghana, dedicated stalls and warehouses in major wholesale markets — 20 years deep in Africa & Middle East trade.
